Chris Lesage – Character TD

My name’s Chris Lesage, and I’m a 3D character rigger and technical artist in Maya and Softimage XSI.

For the past 10 or so years, I’ve offered advanced, animator-friendly rigging, versatile technical support and ad-hoc problem solving throughout the animation pipeline. I also develop time-saving plugins and scripts in Python and PyMEL. I take pride in finding elegant solutions and soaking up new ideas and knowledge from the tech artist world and beyond.
